## Core Features

### Writing & Formatting
- **Markdown Support**: Full GitHub-flavored Markdown with extensions
- **Code Blocks**: Syntax highlighting for 100+ programming languages
- **Math Expressions**: LaTeX-style mathematical notation
- **Diagrams**: Mermaid diagrams and flowcharts
- **Embeds**: Images, videos, and external content

### Organization & Discovery
- **Tags**: Flexible tagging system for categorization
- **Search**: Full-text search across all your memos
- **Filters**: Filter by tags, dates, and content types
- **Archives**: Archive old memos to keep workspace clean

### Sharing & Collaboration
- **Public Sharing**: Share memos with custom URLs
- **Export Options**: Export to Markdown, JSON, or PDF
- **RSS Feeds**: Subscribe to public memo feeds
- **API Access**: Programmatic access to your data

## Best Practices

### Writing Effective Memos
1. **Use descriptive titles** - Make memos easy to find later
2. **Tag consistently** - Develop a tagging strategy that works for you
3. **Break up long content** - Use headings and lists for readability
4. **Link related memos** - Create connections between related ideas

### Organizing Your Workspace
1. **Regular cleanup** - Archive or delete outdated memos
2. **Consistent tagging** - Use a standard set of tags
3. **Backup regularly** - Export your data periodically
4. **Review periodically** - Go through old memos to rediscover ideas
